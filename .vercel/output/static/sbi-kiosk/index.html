SBI Kiosk Banking Services

Access State Bank of India services in your neighborhood

Authorized SBI Customer Service Point

As a State Bank of India Customer Service Point (CSP), Saurav Computer brings essential banking services to the doorstep of the Shendurjan community. Our kiosk banking facility helps bridge the gap between traditional banking and the unbanked population.

Through our services, residents can access banking facilities without traveling to distant bank branches, making financial inclusion a reality for all.

SBI Kiosk Banking Services

Access essential banking services at our authorized SBI Customer Service Point in Shendurjan.

Cash Deposits & Withdrawals

Conveniently deposit or withdraw cash from your SBI accounts without traveling to a branch.

Money Transfers

Transfer funds between SBI accounts securely and instantly.

No-Frills Accounts

Open zero balance accounts with a maximum balance of ₹50,000 and daily transaction limit of ₹10,000.

Credit Cards

Apply for General Purpose Credit Card (GCC) and Kisan Credit Card (KCC) for your financial needs.

Term Deposits

Create and manage term deposits with competitive interest rates.

Loan Services

Access loans against Term Deposit Receipts and other eligible collaterals.

Customer Benefits

  • Extended banking hours beyond traditional bank branches
  • Biometric authentication through electronic thumb impression
  • No cheque book required for transactions
  • Cash-only transactions with simplified procedures
  • Convenient location within the community
  • Personalized assistance with banking queries

Interested in Becoming a CSP?

If you're interested in becoming a State Bank of India Customer Service Point, you'll need the following:

  • 150-200 sq ft outlet in a good location
  • Personal computer with minimum 20 GB hard disk
  • Broadband/VSAT internet connection
  • Webcam for identification verification
  • Printer/scanner for documentation
  • Fingerprint device for biometric authentication
