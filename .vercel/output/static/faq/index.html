Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our courses and services

Browse through our frequently asked questions to find information about our IT courses, SBI Kiosk Banking services, and Aaple Seva Kendra operations. If you don't find what you're looking for, please contact us.

IT Courses

MS-CIT (Maharashtra State Certificate in Information Technology) is a basic computer literacy course recognized by the Government of Maharashtra. You should enroll if you want to gain essential computer skills for employment opportunities, further education, or digital literacy. The certification is widely recognized across Maharashtra for government and private sector jobs.

Our Tally course typically takes 2 months to complete with regular classes. The course covers Tally ERP 9 and Tally Prime features, including GST compliance, inventory management, and financial reporting. We offer flexible timings to accommodate working professionals and students.

No prior knowledge is required for our basic courses like MS-CIT. We start from the fundamentals and gradually progress to more advanced topics. However, for specialized courses like programming languages or advanced Tally modules, basic computer knowledge is beneficial.

For course enrollment, you need a valid ID proof (Aadhaar card, PAN card, etc.), passport-size photographs, and educational qualification documents (varies by course). You'll also need to complete our registration form and pay the course fees.

Yes, we provide basic job placement assistance by sharing information about local job opportunities related to your course. We also help with resume preparation and maintain connections with local businesses for potential placements.

SBI Kiosk Banking

To open a no-frills SBI account, visit our center with your Aadhaar card, a recent photograph, and a mobile number linked to Aadhaar. Our staff will guide you through the biometric verification process and account opening procedure. The entire process takes about 30 minutes.

No-frills accounts have a maximum balance limit of ₹50,000. These accounts also have a daily transaction limit of ₹10,000. If you need higher limits, we can help you upgrade to a regular SBI account through the nearest branch.

Yes, you can withdraw money from any SBI account at our kiosk, provided you are the account holder and complete the necessary biometric verification. The daily withdrawal limit is ₹10,000 for no-frills accounts and may vary for other account types.

Our SBI Kiosk Banking services are available from 9:00 AM to 7:00 PM Monday through Friday, and from 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM on Saturday. This provides extended banking hours compared to traditional bank branches.

Our kiosk primarily facilitates transfers between SBI accounts. For transfers to non-SBI accounts, we recommend using the SBI mobile banking app or visiting the nearest SBI branch for NEFT/RTGS services.

Aaple Seva Kendra

To obtain a 7/12 extract, visit our center with your Aadhaar card, details of the land (survey number, village name), and the required fee. Our staff will process your application through the Aaple Sarkar portal, and you'll receive the certified document as per government processing times.

Through our Aaple Seva Kendra, you can apply for various certificates including income certificates, domicile certificates, caste certificates, birth/death certificates, non-creamy layer certificates, solvency certificates, and residence certificates among others.

Processing times vary depending on the type of certificate. Some certificates like income certificates may be processed in 7-10 working days, while others might take longer based on government verification procedures. We'll provide you with an estimated timeline when you apply.

Yes, you can book Maharashtra State Road Transport Corporation (MSRTC) bus tickets through our Aaple Seva Kendra. Simply provide your journey details, identification, and make the payment to receive your confirmed booking.

The charges include the government fee for the specific service plus a nominal service charge as authorized by MahaOnline. The exact amount varies by service type. We'll provide you with a detailed breakdown of charges before processing your request.
